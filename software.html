Software


bayesCT: Adaptive Bayesian Clinical Trials

bayesCT implements simulation and analysis of adaptive Bayesian clinical trials with early stopping and incorporating historical data.
[CRAN][github][vignette]

blockRAR: Blocked Response-Adaptive Randomization (RAR) Designs

blockRAR implements blocked RAR for both frequentist and Bayesian designs and the final analysis is stratified by block.
[paper][[CRAN][github][vignette]

LPWC: Lag Penalized Weighted Correlation for Time Series Clustering

LPWC implements clustering for short time series data while accounting for lags and irregular time series data points.
[paper][CRAN][github][vignette]

SSNI: Sample Size for Non-Inferiority Trials

SSNI is a shiny app to compute the optimal randomization ratio and sample size for non-inferiority trials.
[paper][github][App]
